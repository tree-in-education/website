Dr. Robot

Produced by Sparklemuffins

Annika Bagazinski, Noa Levi, Maryam Abdullah, Jazmin Santiago, Elizabeth Eakin

Northside College Prep

Artificial intelligence, or AI, has long since made its way into daily conversation, once a sci-fi classic and now an imminent reality. While we are far from true AI, machine learning has made its way into our daily lives, from online shopping to self-driving cars to products like Alexa. As technology evolves and integrates into our lives and even into our healthcare, it's important to examine the impacts and ramifications it will have.
